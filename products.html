GameGoggles™ – YOU ARE THERE

Immersive Wireless Gaming Headgear

GameGoggles™ revolutionize how you interact with virtual worlds by combining immersive visuals, integrated audio, voice interaction, and finger-based control into one sleek, adjustable headset.

Head-Mounted Display:

  • Dual Flip Monitors
    Monitors flip up for instant return to reality — ideal for quick breaks or multi-tasking.
  • Fine Focus Adjustment
    Dial in perfect vision by adjusting the monitor-to-eye distance.
  • Coarse Adjustment System
    Custom-fit bridge for fast fitting and comfort, adaptable to any user.

Integrated Acoustic Headset:

  • Built-in stereo audio delivers immersive sound with noise-isolating cups.
  • Wireless design for a fully untethered experience.

Voice-Enabled Microphone:

  • Embedded directional mic captures your voice clearly — perfect for team chat, game commands, or voice assistants.

Finger Clip Cursor Controller (NEW):

  • Innovative finger-mounted sensor lets you move your cursor, aim, or interact with on-screen elements via subtle finger gestures.
  • Ideal for gamers with limited mobility or who want hands-free interaction.
  • Compatible with PC, consoles, and mobile devices.
